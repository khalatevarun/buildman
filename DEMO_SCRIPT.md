## Delivery Notes

- **Two wow beats:** sandbox spins up instantly + env var card. Slow down at both.
- **Silence is your friend** — after deploying and after the env var card loads, say nothing. Let the UI land.
- **Don't rush the technical lines** — "pool of warm sandbox environments" and "open-source agentic coding CLI" need a slightly slower pace or they blur.
- **Raise your voice on "instantly"** — it's the payoff word for the cold-start setup.
- **Lean back after queuing prompts** — physical confidence sells the async feature better than words.
